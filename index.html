The Process

  • Step 1

    Make an Appointment

    Call or Email Puja, and schedule an appointment!

  • STEP 2

    Check out the inventory!

    Come into our workshop, check out our inventory! We specialize in customized designer outfits, so be sure to tell us about your style, likes and dislikes, and the type of event you are shopping for.

  • STEP 3

    Measurements

    After assessing the type of outfit and style you are looking to achieve, we will take your measurements and create a customer profile.

  • STEP 4

    Designer Consultation

    We will consult with our India-based designers, discuss your measurements, your style, the look you are going for, and create a customized outfit JUST FOR YOU! If you like our work, the order will be processed.

  • Shipped
    and
    Delivered!

Our Amazing Team

Together, they have transformed Indian Fashion in Southern California.

Puja Oberoi

Lead Designer

Arvinder Oberoi

Lead Marketer

Arvinder and Puja started Prathaa Design Studio in 2005. After realizing that most Indian retailers carry the same merchandise, they realized that Southern California is missing the uniqueness that Indian Fashion is known for. By focusing on customized clothing, they have given their customers the distinctive look that makes them stand out. The exclusivity of each article of clothing, coupled with the unparalleled customer service, makes Prathaa the industry leader for Indian designerwear.
